🪙 Token Creator: Now with SPL Classic & Token-2022

Our Token Creator just got a major upgrade. You can now choose between two token standards when creating your Solana token — and every option costs just 0.05 SOL.

Choose Your Standard

When creating a token, you now pick between:

Token-2022 (Recommended)

The newer Solana token program with built-in metadata support. Your token name, symbol, and image appear automatically in all modern wallets and explorers without needing Metaplex.

SPL Token (Classic)

The original Solana token program. Maximum compatibility with every DEX, wallet, and protocol. Uses Metaplex for metadata.

New Features

  • Logo Upload to IPFS — Your token image is uploaded to decentralized storage and linked as metadata. It appears automatically on Solscan, Birdeye, DexScreener, and wallet apps.
  • Immutable Metadata (+0.05 SOL) — Lock your token's name, symbol, and image permanently. Nobody can change it after creation — builds trust with holders.
  • Multi-Wallet Support — Connect with Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, Coinbase Wallet, or Trust Wallet. Works on desktop and mobile.
  • Flat 0.05 SOL per option — Every add-on (freeze revoke, mint revoke, immutable, socials) costs the same: 0.05 SOL. Simple and transparent.

Fee Breakdown

  • Base token creation: 0.05 SOL
  • Disable Freeze Authority: +0.05 SOL
  • Revoke Mint Authority: +0.05 SOL
  • Immutable Metadata: +0.05 SOL
  • Add Social Links: +0.05 SOL

Maximum total: 0.25 SOL for a fully configured token with all options enabled.

How It Works

  • Connect your wallet
  • Choose SPL Token or Token-2022
  • Fill in name, symbol, supply, decimals
  • Upload your logo (automatically stored on IPFS)
  • Select options (freeze revoke, mint revoke, immutable, socials)
  • Click Create — your token is live in 10-30 seconds
  • Add liquidity on Raydium with the provided link
