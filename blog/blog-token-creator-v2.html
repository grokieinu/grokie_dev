🪙 Token Creator v2: Dual Standard & IPFS Image Upload

We just shipped a major upgrade to our Token Creator. You can now choose between two token standards and your logo automatically appears on all explorers and wallets.

What's New

🔧 Choose Your Token Standard

You now have two options when creating a token:

  • Token-2022 (Recommended) — Supports on-chain metadata, extensions, and image URL directly. Your token name, symbol, and logo are visible in all modern wallets and explorers without any additional steps.
  • SPL Token (Classic) — The original Solana token program with maximum compatibility across all DeFi protocols. Best if you need broad support.

🎨 Automatic Logo on Explorers

Upload your logo and it gets stored on IPFS (decentralized storage). The image URL is embedded in your token's metadata, which means your logo automatically appears on:

  • Solscan
  • Birdeye
  • DexScreener
  • Phantom, Solflare, and other wallets

No more manual submissions to get your logo displayed — it just works.

🔒 Immutable Metadata Option

New option to make your metadata permanent. Once set, no one (including you) can change the token name, symbol, or image. This builds trust with your community — they know the branding can never be changed to something misleading.

Updated Fee Structure

We've made pricing simpler and more affordable. Each option is now 0.05 SOL:

  • Base fee: 0.05 SOL (token creation)
  • Disable Freeze Authority: +0.05 SOL
  • Revoke Mint Authority: +0.05 SOL
  • Immutable Metadata: +0.05 SOL
  • Add Social Links: +0.05 SOL

Maximum total: 0.25 SOL for a fully secured token with all options enabled.

Multi-Wallet Support

Connect with any major Solana wallet — not just Phantom. We now support:

  • Phantom
  • Solflare
  • Backpack
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • Trust Wallet

Mobile users can also open the page in their wallet's built-in browser for seamless signing.
